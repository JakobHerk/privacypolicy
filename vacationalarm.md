# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March, 24th 2026

## Overview

This app is designed to function as a local alarm management tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled responsibly and transparently.

## Data Collection

This app does **not collect, store, or share any personal data**. All alarm configurations, including times, repeat schedules, and pause settings, are stored locally on your device.

## Use of Data

Since no personal data is collected, no data is used for analytics, tracking, advertising, or profiling purposes.

## Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for specific functionality:

- **In-App Purchases**: Handled through Apple’s App Store infrastructure.
- **Subscription Management**: Managed securely via Apple and RevenueCat.

These services may process limited, non-identifiable data as required to provide their functionality. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more details.

## Data Sharing

We do **not share any user data** with third parties.

## Data Storage

All user data (such as alarms and settings) is stored **locally on the device** and is not transmitted to external servers.

## User Rights

Because no personal data is collected or stored externally, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete beyond what is already available within the app itself.

## Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since no personal data is collected at all, it is safe for general audiences.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated revision date.
